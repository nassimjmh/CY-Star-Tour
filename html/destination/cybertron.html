Cybertron

Presentation

Welcome to Cybertron – The Beating Heart of the Universe!

Discover the awe-inspiring world of Cybertron, a planet of towering metallic landscapes, luminous cities, and cutting-edge technology.

From the majestic spires of Iacon to the endless energy fields of the Sea of Rust, Cybertron offers a perfect fusion of innovation and history.

Whether you're here to witness the wisdom of the Primes or explore the vast labyrinths of ancient Cybertronian tunnels, adventure awaits on this ever-evolving mechanical wonder. Come and experience the power and grandeur of Cybertron – where every skyline tells a story of legends!

Schedule

Day 1

Lacon

    Begin your adventure in lacon, the vibrant heart of Cybertron.

    Enjoy a guided tour of the futuristic city, complete with holographic projections and interactive exhibits showcasing Cybertron's rich history and advanced technology !

Energon Hunt

    Explore your surroundings as if you’re searching for hidden Energon sources.

    Use reality app and take creative photos of anything that looks like advanced Cybertronian tech.

Day 2

Transformation Training

    Cybertronians can shift forms, so practice adaptive thinking—switch between two distinct skills (e.g., drawing vs. speed typing, running vs. solving puzzles) to improve mental flexibility.

Choose Your Faction

    Will you be an Autobots or a Decepticons ?

Choose wisely, because you will fight against the other team in physical games !

Day 3

Exploration

    The Grand Iacon Archives: Visit a real-life library or online archive and research something new (science, history, AI). Summarize your findings in English, as if reporting to Cybertron’s High Council.

Strategy Games

    Engage in a strategy game (chess, a tactical video game, or even a mental challenge) to develop your Cybertronian battle instincts.

    Think like an Autobot or Decepticon!

Day 4

The Botanical Gardens

   Take a serene stroll through Cybertron’s magnificent botanical gardens.

    These lush landscapes blend organic growth with futuristic technology, offering visitors the chance to see both nature and innovation intertwined.

Farewell Spaceport

    End your journey at Cybertron’s futuristic spaceport.

    Relax in luxury lounges, enjoy panoramic views of the planet, and grab a souvenir.
