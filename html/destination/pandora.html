Pandora

Presentation

Welcome to Pandora, a mysterious and captivating exomoon, where breathtaking landscapes, extraordinary wildlife, and the rich culture of the Na'vi await.

Explore iconic sites like the sacred Tree of Souls, the floating Hallelujah Mountains, and the towering Hometree, home to the Omaticaya tribe.
Pandora promises an unforgettable adventure filled with wonder and discovery.

Embark on an unforgettable journey to Pandora, the legendary city of the Na'vi, with this carefully crafted 4-day program.
Each day is packed with unique activities to immerse you in the wonders of this extraordinary planet.

Schedule

Day 1

Bioluminescent Forest Exploration

   Start your day with a hike through the bioluminescent forest. Discover glowing plants and observe nocturnal creatures that come to life in the soft light of the flora.

Visit to the Tree of Souls

   After a light lunch, head to the Tree of Souls, Pandora's most sacred site.
   Take time to meditate and connect with the surrounding nature, feeling the spiritual energy that emanates from this iconic place.

Day 2

Hot Air Balloon Tour

   Embark on a hot air balloon ride over the Hallelujah Mountains.
   Marvel at the floating rock formations and celestial waterfalls from above.

Banshee Observation

   After lunch, participate in a Banshee observation session.
    Learn about the habits and unique characteristics of these majestic aerial dragons as you watch them in their natural habitat.

Day 3

Meet the Na'vi

   Spend the morning with the Na'vi, Pandora's indigenous people. Participate in workshops to learn more about their way of life, traditions, and language.

Hometree Visit

   After a shared meal with the Na'vi, visit Hometree, the ancestral home of the Omaticaya tribe.
   Explore the different levels of this colossal tree and discover how the Na'vi live in harmony with nature.

Day 4

Crystal Waterfalls Hike

   On your final day, hike to the Crystal Waterfalls.
   Enjoy the beauty of the shimmering waterfalls and bathe in the surrounding natural pools.

Wildlife Safari

   End your adventure with a wildlife photography safari.
   Capture images of Hexapedes, Viperwolves, and other fascinating creatures in their natural environment.
