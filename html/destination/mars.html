Mars

Presentation

Travel to Mars, the Reimagined Red Planet! Welcome to Galactic Horizons, your interstellar travel agency.

Forget earthly beaches and embark on an extraordinary adventure to Mars, where science fiction becomes reality!
Set off on an incredible journey to Mars and experience something truly unique.

Let yourself be transported beyond earthly boundaries and explore a fascinating new world!

Schedule

Day 1

Olympus Mons

    Begin your adventure with an exploration of Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the Solar System, standing 25 km high
   Despite its immense size, its gentle slopes make it an accessible climb, offering breathtaking panoramic views.

Relaxation

    Relax at the base camp and enjoy interactive talks on Mars’ volcanic history and geology.

Day 2

Valles Marineris

    Venture into Valles Marineris, the largest canyon in the Solar System, stretching over 3,000 km
   This natural wonder remains a mystery, offering a chance to explore its deep valleys and dramatic landscapes.

Guided Trek

   Join a guided trek along the canyon’s ridges, where experts will explain the leading theories about its formation and evolution over millions of years.

Day 3

Gale Crater

    Explore Gale Crater, famous for being the landing site of NASA’s Curiosity rover in 2012.

   Scientists believe this area once contained liquid water!

Mount Sharp

   Start your hike towards Mount Sharp, a massive peak in the center of the crater, where layered rock formations reveal Mars' climatic history.

Day 4

The Tharsis Volcanic Region

    Conclude your journey with a visit to the Tharsis volcanic plateau, home to 12 massive volcanoes spread across 4,000 km.

Interactive workshop

Participate in an interactive workshop on Martian volcanology, where you’ll learn about the forces that shaped these giants and their role in the planet’s evolution.
