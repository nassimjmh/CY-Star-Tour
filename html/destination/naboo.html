Naboo

Presentation

Welcome to Naboo – A Hidden Gem in the Galaxy!

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Naboo, a planet of lush green landscapes, shimmering lakes, and stunning architecture.

From the vibrant city of Theed to the peaceful waters of Lake Paonga, Naboo offers a perfect blend of nature and culture.

Whether you're here to explore ancient Gungan cities or relax in royal gardens, adventure awaits on this serene paradise. Come and experience the elegance and tranquility of Naboo – where every sunset is a masterpiece!

Schedule

Day 1

Royal Elegance

   Explore Theed Royal Palace, marveling at its golden domes and intricate architecture.

    Discover the history of Naboo’s noble past !

Sunset Serenity

   Take a scenic sunset cruise on Lake Paonga, where the sky reflects in the shimmering waters as you enjoy a peaceful ride.

Day 2

Peaceful Morning

    Enjoy a break ! You really deserve it

Mystical Waters

   Dive into the Gungan Underwater City of Otoh Gunga.

    Walk through glowing bubble-like halls, meet the locals, and experience a unique aquatic culture.

Day 3

Safari

   Ride a Kaadu safari through Naboo’s rolling plains, spotting exotic creatures and feeling the wind rush past you.

Cliffs

    Stargaze from the Varykino Cliffs, where the galaxy unfolds above the tranquil countryside.

Day 4

Ancient Secrets

   Hike to the Lost Temple of Shaak-Ran, an ancient ruin hidden in the jungle, whispering stories of Naboo’s forgotten past.

Farewell Feast

   Enjoy a grand farewell banquet in Theed, savoring traditional Naboo cuisine under the soft glow of lanterns.
